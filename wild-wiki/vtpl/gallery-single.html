Animal details:

The lion (Panthera leo) is one of the most iconic and majestic big cats, known for its strength, beauty, and ferocity. Lions are native to various parts of Africa and Asia and are recognized for their distinctive golden fur and impressive manes in males.

They are social animals, living in groups known as prides, which typically consist of several related females, their offspring, and a few adult males

Until the lion learns how to write, every story will glorify the hunter

Lions are apex predators and formidable hunters, primarily targeting large herbivores such as zebras, wildebeests, and buffalo. They use stealth and teamwork to stalk and ambush their prey, often hunting together in coordinated efforts.

Despite their reputation as fearsome predators, lions are also known for their affectionate social bonds within their pride, engaging in grooming, play, and communal resting
